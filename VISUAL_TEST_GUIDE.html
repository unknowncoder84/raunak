👤 BUYER FLOW

1 Login as Buyer
buyer1@test.com
buy01
2 Browse & Add to Cart
• Browse products on home page
• Click "Add to Cart" on any product
• Cart icon shows item count
3 Checkout
• Click cart icon (top right)
• Review items
• Click "Proceed to Checkout"
• Fill shipping address
• Click "Place Order"
4 View Your Orders
• Click profile menu (top right)
• Select "My Orders"
• ✅ YOUR ORDER APPEARS HERE!
• See: Product, Status, Amount, Date
• Auto-refreshes every 10 seconds
5 Track Order Status
• Filter by status (All, Pending, Shipped, etc.)
• Watch status change as seller processes
• Get notifications for updates

🏪 SELLER FLOW

1 Login as Seller
Seller1@test.com
user1
⚠️ Note the capital 'S' in Seller!
2 View Dashboard
• Land on Seller Dashboard
• ✅ BUYER'S ORDER APPEARS HERE!
• See in "Pending Orders" section
• Auto-refreshes every 3 seconds
3 Accept Order
• Click "Accept Order" button
• Order moves to "Ready to Ship"
• Buyer gets notification
• Product added to your inventory
4 Ship Order
• Click "Mark as Shipped"
• Order status → Shipped
• Buyer sees update in "My Orders"
• Tracking info added
5 Complete & Earn
• Order delivered → Status: Delivered
• Payment released to your wallet
• Earnings shown in dashboard
• Buyer can leave review
